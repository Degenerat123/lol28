Bloodhound Gang (Bloodhound Gang): Biography of the group

The idea of ​​creating the group belonged to the young vocalist Jimmy Pop, nee James Moyer Franks, and musician-guitarist Daddy Logn Legs, better known as Daddy Long Legs, who later left the group.

Basically, the theme of the group's songs is connected with rude jokes about intimate relationships and everything connected with them. The musicians work in the genre of comedy rock. Nevertheless, compositions periodically appear in the genres of rapcore, nu-metal, hip-hop rap. There are a number of crossovers with other artists. The Bloodhound Gang is known for their provocative and shocking behavior, even hooliganism.
